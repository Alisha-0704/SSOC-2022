FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Hacktoberfest?

The Hacktoberfest is an initiative of the company DigialOcean to encourage people to contribute to open source projects. After sending 5 pull requests you will receive a limited edition Hacktoberfest 2020 t-shirt at home. You can also take the greenway and plant trees instead of the t-shirt.

What if I'm new?

Do not worry! These events are precisely designed to help novice people overcome the barrier of early contributions. There will be people of all levels and for sure you will be able to learn a lot.

What projects can I contribute to? Do they need to be issues marked with the Hacktoberfest tag?

To anyone on Github. We will prepare a series of specific projects, in which we will have mentors to facilitate collaboration.

It is not necessary that the issues you work on are marked with th Hacktoberfest. tag . Itâ€™s simply a way to do them in order to help people who work autonomously find them.

I would like to propose a project. Do I have to notify in advance or can I do so directly on the day of the initial event?

It is preferable to propose this in advance by writing to contact@bauddhikgeeks.tech . This will give us time to take a look at them and prepare them together with you to make it easy to collaborate on them.

Do I have to sign up somewhere to receive my t-shirt?

Yes, you have to sign up on the official DigialOcean website of Hacktoberfest.

What language will be spoken at the virtual events?

The events will take place mainly in English.

What dedication of work is expected to the projects during the month?

Apart from the day of the start of the event, the working groups will decide their own rules regarding dedication. They can work every day, only stay on the weekends to do a sprint ...
